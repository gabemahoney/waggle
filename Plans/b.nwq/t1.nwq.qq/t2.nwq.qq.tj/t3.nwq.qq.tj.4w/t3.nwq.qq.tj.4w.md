## Context

T3-C rebuilds the tmux launch composition. This subtask extends `tests/test_engine_spawn_worker_options.py` with the coverage for that surface: built-in env vars, conditional `_MODEL` label, merged `extra_env`, merged `claude_status_labels`, `HOME=<claude_home>`, start-directory flag, and the conditional command-line composition (`--model`, `--effort`, `--settings`, `claude_args`).

## What Needs to Change

Extend `tests/test_engine_spawn_worker_options.py` with a `TestLaunchComposition` class (or similar) covering:

- **Unconditional env set:** confirm every SR-4.2 unconditional env var is present on the `new-session` argv for a baseline spawn (e.g. `CLAUDE_STATUS_INSTANCE_ID`, `CLAUDE_STATUS_RELAY_MODE=on`, `CLAUDE_STATUS_AUQ_MODE=record`, `CLAUDE_STATUS_LABEL_CLAUDE_SPAWN_OWNED=1`, `_SESSION_NAME`, `_CWD`).
- **Conditional `_MODEL` label:** one test confirms presence when `model="opus"` is supplied (value lowercased to `opus`); one test confirms absence when `model` is unsupplied.
- **start-directory:** confirm the `new-session` argv carries the tmux start-directory flag (e.g. `-c <resolved cwd>`) and that the send-keys payload does NOT include a `cd` preamble.
- **`extra_env` flow-through:** entries appear as `-e KEY=VALUE` in the new-session argv.
- **`claude_status_labels` uppercased:** caller passes a bare lower-cased label key; the impl emits `-e CLAUDE_STATUS_LABEL_<UPPERCASED_KEY>=<VALUE>`.
- **`claude_home`:** when supplied, `-e HOME=<claude_home>` appears in the new-session argv.
- **Conditional `--model`:** present in send-keys iff `model` supplied.
- **Conditional `--effort`:** present in send-keys iff `thinking` supplied; the level value matches the resolved `thinking`.
- **`claude_args` verbatim:** every entry in the resolved `claude_args` list appears verbatim in the send-keys command, in order, after the last Claude-Spawn-emitted flag.
- **Explicit `tmux_session_name` override:** when the caller supplies `tmux_session_name=`, that exact value is used for `-s` and for the send-keys target.

Tests use `tests.helpers.fake_claude_status` for any incidental Claude Status seam.
