- Replace every "Waggle" / "waggle" occurrence with "Claude Spawn" / `claude-spawn` per the noun/identifier conventions in the PRD's identifier table.

### `docs/architecture/*.md`
- Replace every "Waggle" / "waggle" occurrence with the new project name and the corresponding identifier (`claude-spawn`, `claude_spawn`, `Claude Spawn`).
