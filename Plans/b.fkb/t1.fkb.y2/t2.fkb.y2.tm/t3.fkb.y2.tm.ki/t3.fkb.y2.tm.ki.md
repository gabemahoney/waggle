## Context
The Engineer is flipping the default tmux session prefix in `spawn_worker_impl` from `waggle-` to `spawn-`. Tests in `tests/test_engine_spawn_worker.py` currently assert on the `waggle-` prefix and use it in inline comments documenting the expected default-name shape. This subtask updates the test assertions and comments in lockstep so the suite remains green.

## What Needs to Change

There are roughly four sites in the default-session-name tests: a `startswith("waggle-")` assertion, a comment describing the shape, and two `f"waggle-{...}"` expected-value expressions.
- Preserve the test's pass-through case (the test that confirms a caller-provided `session_name` is used verbatim) — that test doesn't check the `waggle-`/`spawn-` prefix and is unchanged.
- DO NOT modify label-key assertions, env-var-name assertions, or any other test method renamed by Epic 3 (those landed in Epic 3 and are already in their final form).
